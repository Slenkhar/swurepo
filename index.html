How to Play

Star Wars: Unlimited TCG is a fast-paced, back and forth action, deck construction (sometimes called deckbuilding) card game that sees you pit your favourite Star Wars "Leader" against an opponent's. Although very easy to learn, the inherent variance of such card games provides a lot of room for strategic and tactical development as you become more and more familiar with the card pool, meta and play patterns.
